# AGENTS.md

## Project Goal
Create a 30-second cinematic narrative AI-video commercial for eyeglasses.
The result must feel premium, emotionally engaging, visually coherent, and production-ready.

## Core Principle
This is not a generic fashion ad.
The eyeglasses are not a secondary accessory — they are the semantic and visual turning point of the film.

## Non-Negotiables
- One main character.
- High consistency of face identity, age appearance, body proportions, and movement language.
- High consistency of eyeglasses geometry, material, color, and fit.
- High consistency of location logic, architectural language, and light behavior.
- No random change of time of day, wardrobe, props, accessories, or emotional tone.
- No generic influencer aesthetics.
- No meaningless beauty shots without narrative function.

## Story Rules
- Every shot must have a narrative purpose.
- The film must move from internal noise / uncertainty to clarity / precision / presence.
- The glasses must function as the emotional and visual turning point.
- The pacing should feel cinematic, deliberate, and premium.
- The final film should be edit-friendly, not just visually impressive in isolated frames.
